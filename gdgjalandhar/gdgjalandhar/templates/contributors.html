Contributor to GDG Mumbai

· As a GDG Mumbai Contributor you will get a chance to organize the various events, meetups, seminar and code labs conducted throught theyear by GDG Mumbai. You will be expected to give your sincere dedication in Event Management work like Techincal, Creative, Logistics, Photography, Website & Social media, Crowd Management and Documentation.

· The GDG Mumbai Contributors will also be expected to take an active participation for the pre and post-event work like Publicity, Event requirements or other event related work at which they would like to.

· The Contributors will be graced with the following on successful completion of their term:

  • 1. First Hand Chance to interact with community and speakers and Googlers attending the event
  • 2. Opportunity to increase your people skills
  • 3. Get exposure to recent technology
  • 4. Opportunity to organize large events
  • 5. Letter of Recommendation
  • 6. Certificate of Appreciation

· The duration of your membership as a Contributor would be of 1 year and after which your performance would be accurately evaluated.
