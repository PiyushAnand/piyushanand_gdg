Event Description

GDG DevFests is a global campaign that will offer speaker sessions across Google product areas and various rechnologies. GDG DevFest Mumbai 2013 will be inspired by and uniquely tailored to the needs of the student and developer community. To its core, this event will be powered by a shared belief that when developers come together to exchange ideas, amazing things can happen.

This event will be a great opportunity to learn more about Google technologies and developer products. The events also give one a chance to meet developer advocates and engineers who work on these products and ask them any questions one might have. GDG Mumbai is proud to host the DevFest this year again. The conference will have introductory, medium level talks on Google developer products which will help community users to get familiarize with these technologies like App Engine, Web Services and Android.
