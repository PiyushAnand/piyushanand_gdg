Vipul Shah

Vipul Shah

Vipul Shah started his career as an Android development in Dec 2010 and in these 3 years he has worked on quite a few awesome Industrial projects and also delivered talks on Android Development.

Currently he is working as an Senior Android Engineer with vserv.mobi. At vserv.mobi his tasks includes doing architecture design & coding for advertising SDK in various mobile smartphone platforms.

On weekends when he is not coding he figure out how to create and teach Android technical content so college and Industry folks learn it easier, faster and better.







Priyanka Agrawal

Priyanka Agrawal

Priyanka is the digital strategist and co-owner at fractalink design studio pvt. ltd. With close to 14 years in the field of design, she has been instrumental in converting chaos into organised data and impossible requirements and briefs into meaningful user journeys for her clientele and delight for the users.

She started here career as an Architect and went on to pursue her love for UX and UI by acquiring a master's degree in Graphic Design from Middlesex University in London. Post her graduation she joined one of the leading agencies DMGT (Daily Mail Group Trust) in London as a UX and UI designer.

With a strong motivation to bring about a change in the field of UX and UI design and help propagate the role of user experience in the Digital Design domain in India she returned back to start up on her own.

Since then she has been instrumental in shaping up some of the biggest brand in India and helped them create delightful experiences on various digital platforms, which includes Andriod, Windows 8, iOS and web.

She has also been closely working with Samsung and Microsoft shape some of their initiatives in their respective platforms.

With close to around 4 years now in India she has helped companies across various verticals which includes BFSI, Data Analytics, Entertainment, e-commerce to come onboard. Some of them include Disney, ICICI, Dun and Bradstreet, Hungama Digital, Flipkart, Myntra.

With wealth of experience which she gathered during her journey, she conducts UX workshops across India as a way of giving back to the community. Priyanka has been an active mentor on several startup communities like iAccelerator (IIM Ahemdabad), IQ bootcamp (Mumbai), TiE (Mumbai) where she regularly conducts workshops. Her objective is to instill the idea of, how good UX marks the base of a good digital product among young startup communities.

In her own small way she wants to bring about a change in the social structure of India and the perception of women and the do's and don'ts associated with them. She believes and thrives on the philosophy of 'Be the change you want to see'.

Romin Irani

Romin Irani

Romin Irani loves learning about new technologies and teaching it to others. He has been developing software on a variety of platforms over the last 19 years, spanning desktop, web and mobile technologies. He is a Google Developer Expert Cloud for 2014. He blogs regularly at http://www.rominirani.com and you can follow him on Twitter @iRomin











Yogi Udgire

Yogi Udgire

He is a Serial Entrepreneur. He is the digital lead for Institute of Digital Marketing initiative of Rishi Group in India. After his Engineering Graduation coupled with a Management degree launching his career as Management Trainee with Central Govt Company worked in multiple disciplines of Projects, Technical & Materials, followed by a Start-up venture at IIT Bombay then choosing being a Entrepreneur in IT, Biotech & Wellness over last 18 years.









Josan Dometita

Josan Dometita

Josan Astrid Dometita is an HTML5 enthusiast and Google Developers Group Community Manager from the Philippines. Having the interest in the complex ins and outs of computer technology made her earn her Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, with a major in Software Engineering. While still in school, she started freelancing online as a web developer, and expanded her portfolio of skills to become a vector artist and motion graphics animator. She started working as a Multimedia Developer in Manila and together with her friends, then built their own consulting and startup company, Aestrea Software Development, where she is the Creative Director. She calls herself codesigner (coder/designer), a hybrid name that could best describe her skill set, bridging the gap between both the design and development worlds.




Jatin Malhotra

Jatin Malhotra

He is highly enthusiastic and passionate about everything related to technology. He has recently completed his Bachelor’s in Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering. He is the Founder and Head of Google Developer Group – Mumbai. Before, as a Lead of Student Outreach Program of Asus in India, he has worked with Engineering Colleges in fields like Technical Evangelism, Research Partnerships,Technology Accessibility. He has been associated with Microsoft as a msp from 2009-2012. He currently is the Head of Western Region of India for Yuva – Microsoft Initiative.

He has been Awarded by the 11th President of India, Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam for the brilliant work in evangelizing technology and spreading digital literacy among the underprivileged in the country. Also currently, he is involved with International Federation of Medical Associations’ and is working on implementation of recent technology in Health Care in various countries across the world.
