What is Google Cloud Camp?

Google Cloud Workshop aims to give tools and skills to develop ideas into a project utilizing advanced Google mobile, social, cloud and web technologies.

What is the sole purpose of organising GCCM?

The target of the program is to give a hands-on experience with modern technologies, work in small teams, develop innovative projects and applications, and face real world challenges. The program also helps universities produce better graduates, more suited to work in the industry, and specifically in the areas of web/mobile/social/cloud development. Professionals and students will acquire the following skills: Product Design including User Interface & User Experience design, working in teams with a tight schedule and hard deadlines, project management, working with new technologies while learning on the go, and more.
