Vipul Shah

Vipul Shah

Vipul Shah started his career as an Android development in Dec 2010 and in these 3 years he has worked on quite a few awesome Industrial projects and also delivered talks on Android Development.

Currently he is working as an Technology Consultant with Deloitte Digital. At Deloitte Digital his tasks includes doing architecture design & coding for advertising SDK in various mobile smartphone platforms.

On weekends when he is not coding he figure out how to create and teach Android technical content so college and Industry folks learn it easier, faster and better.







Jonathan Pereira

Jonathan Pereira

Jonathan Pereira, being the GDG Goa manager, is a fan of Google technologies. However, he was excited about Cloud Computing since it first became a reality mainly because it offered flexibility and power that was unheard of. Google joining the Cloud league with its Cloud Platform only inspired him further to experiment with the platform.

During the day (and the night also), he is an entrepreneur dealing with Web and mobile services in the Cloud. When not working he is usually trying to foster the start-up and tech environment where he thrives. To keep experimenting in life, he likes to travel a lot and attend various conferences not limited to tech.

Parag Naik

Parag Naik

9+ years of experience working with multiple clients and on array of technologies including Android. I spend most of my day architecting an Android App, working with designers to create a rich user experience.
