FAQ

Q: How many people are allowed per team?
A: Ideally we expect Five but if more then do let us know.

Q: What if I don’t have a team to sign up with? Can I sign up alone?
A: Yes! However, since we think you’ll enjoy this so much more with teammates, if you don’t have a team, we can help you join one the day of the event.

Q: How will I know if I’ve been accepted to participate in the Hackathon?
A: If you have registered, you can come down and start building cool stuff.

Q: Do I need to be physically at the Hackathon to participate?
A: Yes. No online option to compete will be offered.

Q: Do I need to bring my own laptop to the event?
A: Yes.

Q: Does it cost anything to participate?
A: It's completely free.

Q: Does GDG get to keep my code or idea?
A: No – what you create is all yours, you own it. We have no intention or desire to use your submission for our business and we are not even keeping copies of the submissions after the event. However, due to the nature of the business environment we operate in, we have to take measures to avoid potential future conflicts for any user generated content created at our events. To address this, we ask that participants grant us a nonexclusive license as a measure of protection.

The purpose and goal is simple: to create a fun environment, foster new ideas, and meet new talent.

Q: Am I guaranteed spot at the event if I register?
A: Unfortunately there is limited space, so we may have to limit the number of amazingly talented folks we let in as per first come first serve basis.

Q: Will you be hiring people from this event?
A: Yes. Our startup partners affiliated with us would definitely offer internship opportunity/jobs/business opportunities to talented and interested folks.

Q: How do I contact you for more information?
A: gdgmumbai@gmail.com, jatin.malhotra@oracle.com, mh@strtup.net
