Uttam Tripathi

Uttam Tripathi

He is working as a Program Manager, Developer Relations at Google India. He has working experience in various companies and organizations including Booz & Company, FHNW, and Amazon.com. Certain technologies including Distributed Systems, Management Consulting, E-commerce, Business Analysis, Program Management, Cloud Computing comes under his skills & expertise.

Sreeraman Thiagarajan

Sreeraman Thiagarajan

Sreeraman Thiagarajan is a passionate marketer by education & profession; he specializes in creating conversations between brands and consumers by leveraging technology. He holds a master degree in marketing & management from IUP, USA. Was an entrepreneur once, now an intraprenuer at Media2win and chapter manager at GBG Mumbai

Jatin Malhotra

Jatin Malhotra

He is highly enthusiastic and passionate about everything related to technology. He has recently completed his Bachelor’s in Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering. He is the Founder and Head of Google Developer Group – Mumbai. Before, as a Lead of Student Outreach Program of Asus in India, he has worked with Engineering Colleges in fields like Technical Evangelism, Research Partnerships,Technology Accessibility.He has been associated with Microsoft as a msp from 2009-2012. He currently is the Head of Western Region of India for Yuva – Microsoft Initiative. Has been Awarded by the 11th President of India, Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam for the brilliant work in evangelizing technology and spreading digital literacy among the underprivileged in the country. Also currently, he is involved with International Federation of Medical Associations’ and is working on implementation of recent technology in Health Care in various countries across the world
